Eddie Van Halen

A tribute to one of the greatest rock guitarists of all time.

Here are a few key events from a life of rock 'n' roll:

  • 1955 - Edward Lodewijk Van Halen was born on January 26th 1955 in Amsterdam, Netherlands.
  • 1962 - Moves to the United States with his family, settling in California.
  • 1964 - Wins first place in the annual piano competition held at Long Beach City College, despite not being able to read music.
  • 1972 - Forms a band with his brother Alex Van Halen on drums.
  • 1978 - The band's self-titled debut album is released and is commercially successful. The recording includes many guitar techniques which Eddie Van Halen either invented or popularised, such as tapping and use of a locking tremolo for extreme pitch changes. and is considered revolutionary by many guitarists.
  • 1981 - Marries Valerie Bertinelli.
  • 2007 - Enters rehab, becoming sober a year later.
  • 2009 - Marries second wife Janie Liszewski.
  • 2019 - Recieves treatment for throat cancer.
  • 2020 - Dies from a stroke on October 6th 2020 at the age of 65.

These are just a few key events from a life full of incident and acheivement.
